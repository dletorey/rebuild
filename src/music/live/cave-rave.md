We traveled to Castleton in the Peak District to go see The Orb and System 7 play live in a cave.

The cave in question is known as the [Devil's Arse](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Peak_Cavern), which is the largest natural cave entrance in Britain. It is also now one of the best venue's that I have ever attended a gig, if you get the chance to go to a gig here do it!
