I have no idea what this is going to be, Chase & Status's marketing says:

> Section 63 from [@chaseandstatus](https://www.instagram.com/p/DXJfBnrlGrb/) - a world-first, groundbreaking immersive experience that blurs the line between the crowd and the chaos. This is not a show, it’s an experience.

Well last night's gig was **not**, [groundbreaking new immersive production](https://magazinelondon.co.uk/listing/chase-status-section-63/), as it was advertised.

They did play live but there was no musicians or singers like in previous but therewas an MC.

The crowd were excellent though and eere thoroughly enjoying themselves.

The venue, Magazine, was pretty cool. There was a good light show and the quality of the sound system was superb, just not loud enough by a long way.

Around the venue there were raised platforms, VIP areas. There was also a car behind barrier which a few times flames came out of it.

The event promised _ground breaking_ and _immersive_, it was just a big room with a car that occasionally burst into flames. This was nothing like or even close to [Arcadia](https://www.arcadiaspectacular.com/) ( where Chase and Status have previously performed). While I do understand thst you can't have giant fire breathing spiders indoors in London they still did not even come close.

Their live shows used to be something very special this was just teo blokes stood behind a desk with a guy shouting "London let's rave!", but the crowd loved it.
