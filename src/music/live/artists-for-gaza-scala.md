This is the fourth time I have attended a gig in support of Gaza & Palestine, the other 3 being:

- [Gig for Gaza](https://www.mojo4music.com/articles/stories/paul-weller-primal-scream-review/) [Brixton Academy](/tags/brixton-academy/) December 2024, featuring Kneecap, Paul Weller, Paloma Faith, Primal Scream and many more.
- [Artist for Gaza](https://whynow.co.uk/read/artists-for-gaza-2025-line-up-announced) Century Club London July 2025, Featuring Paul Weller, Bobby Gillespie, Paul Simonon, Jerry Dammers and loads more.
- [Together for Palestine](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Together_for_Palestine) Wembley Arena September 2025, featuring loads of [performers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Together_for_Palestine#Performers). The full show (4 hours) is [available to watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b_BYSh8SkWs).

## Scala Band

The Scala Band was made up of Madness, Andy McKay (Saxophonist from Roxy Music), Paul Weller amonst others and Bobby Gillespie (Smiling and Dancing at the side of the stage).

There were a number of Madness songs performed, My Girl, Our House, It Must Be Love. They also performed Love Is A Drug by Roxy Music and Paul Weller sang Shipbuilding by Robert Wyatt.

## Healthcare Workers for Palestine

The event was organised to raised funds for [Healthcare Workers for Palestine](https://www.healthworkers4palestine.com/). The Doctors and Nurses in Gaza who continue to turn up for work even though most of their Hospitals and Work places have been destroyed.
