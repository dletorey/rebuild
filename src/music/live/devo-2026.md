On the way in to The O2 I spotted [Guadi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gaudi_(musician)) having a cigarette before he went in. 

There was a merch stand outside with a huge queue. I straight off ignored it as I wasn’t really interested. On walking in I noticed someone wearing a [DEVO Energy Dome](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Energy_dome) a red pyramid hat. Now I have an issue, there’s a red hat I don’t own. When we got into the venue I saw someone wearing one and enquired how much they’d paid, £40!

## Lene Lovich

[Lene Lovich](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lene_Lovich) had already started by the time we’d got in, but I was confident that they’d not played their hit [Lucky Number](https://song.link/i/1023020315), I was right. This track brings back memories of youth club discos (sorry kids the tories got rid of youth clubs which is why you have to play with knives). 

## The Rezillos

[The Rezillos](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Rezillos) also played their hit [Top of the Pops](https://song.link/i/576801139). The were famous for making songs about b-movies, robots, e.t.c.
