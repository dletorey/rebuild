Today we decided to escape London and go for a walk in the out doors.

We parked the car by the [One Garden](https://www.onegardenbrighton.com/), before heading out into the park we went to toilets. On the way to the toilet we passed a food van called [Tallow Street Food](https://tallowstreetfood.co.uk/). We both had a Korean Pork Belly bun, it was similar to a Bhan Mi in a brioche bun, which was delicious.

After a short browse around an upcycle/antique stall we headed out to go for a walk.

The was a map in the car park and from that we decided to walk up the hill to walk through the woods. The weather was great although a tad blustery due to [Storm Dave](https://www.metoffice.gov.uk/about-us/news-and-media/media-centre/weather-and-climate-news/2026/amber-wind-warning-for-easter). When we went into the woods there was a welcome break from the wind.

One of the best things about the walk was a stroll through [an apple orchard](https://brightonpermaculture.org.uk/our-projects/orchards-in-stanmer-park/) of vintage apple varieties.

We there lot of flowers and blossoms to see:

- [Common Bluebell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hyacinthoides_non-scripta)
- [Wood Sorrel](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Oxalis)
- [Wood Anemone](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Anemonoides_nemorosa)
- [Common Dandelion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taraxacum_officinale)
- [Red Dead-nettle](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lamium_purpureum)
- [Daisy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bellis_perennis)
- [Three-cornered Leek](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Allium_triquetrum) - which I thought was snowdrops
- [Tulips](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tulip) - small and red
