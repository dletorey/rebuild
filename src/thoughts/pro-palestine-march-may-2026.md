Today in there were two Marches in London. The Tommy Ten Names, United the Kingdom, far-right march. The one I attended a Pro-Palestine and Anti-Far-Right March.

The last time Tommy Ten Names held a march was September 2025 and it was reported that there were 150,000 attendees. I called the _Rivers of Piss_ march as they had started drinking in the morning and then realised there was no toilets on the route and just pissed in the streets. This time it appeared that Tommy Ten names couldn't attract even a quarter of the previous number, perhaps we should start calling him **Tommy No Mates**.

In comparison the Pro-Palestine march had about 250,000 people. The march started on Exhibition Road, South Kensington, outside the Natural History Museum. We marched past Harrods, Hyde Park Corner, along Piccadilly and left the march at Green Park to go meet my friend at Kings Cross.
