I took Susan to AngloThai to celebrate her 60th birthday.

## Discovery

In March this year I was watching [MasterChef the Professionals](https://www.bbc.co.uk/food/programmes/m002r6p2) and the chefs visited [AngloThai](https://anglothai.co.uk/) to go work in the kitchen. The restaurant is owned my a British/Thai chef who learned Thai cuisine in Bangkok before returning to the UK to open the restaurant.

The restaurant cooked Thai food but with a focus on British ingredients, this meant that instead of rice we had bulgur wheat.

Before the end of the episode of MasterChef I had already booked a table.

## Staff

I was contacted a few days before the booking to make sure that I still wanted the reservation. After confirming I was asked if we were celebrating a special occasion, I told them that it was part of my partner's 60th birthday celebrations. They asked fro her name so that they could write her a birthday card.

I arrived at the restaurant before Susan as she had a meeting to attend, when I arrived they showed me to the table and there was already a birthday card. While waiting I looked at the drinks menu and ordered a _Prickly Ash Negroni_, to be ready on Susan's arrival. The cocktail was  made with:

- Sapling gin
- Nettle Vermouth
- Campari

When Susan arrived all the staff greeted her and wished her happy birthday.

This is a truly wonderful restaurant and I highly recommend that you go if you ever get the chance.

## Menu

The menu was a tasting menu, we were also offered a wine pairing. They partnered with an Austria vineyard. We choose not to have the wine pairing but Susan did have a white Austrian wine to go with her meal and I had a [Sabro Galaxy DDH IPA](https://untappd.com/b/brulo-sabro-galaxy-ddh-ipa/4079983) Non-Alcoholic IPA.

Both Susan & I's favourite dish was the **Chalk Stream Trout**, but every dish was amazing.
