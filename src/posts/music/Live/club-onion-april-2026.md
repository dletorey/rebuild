## Venue

This time the venue was the [The Chapel](https://theangelmicrobrewery.co.uk/thechapel/) above the [Microbrewery pub The Angel](https://maps.app.goo.gl/wYxQ9Z23ja7NbKac7) home of [Angel Beers](https://untappd.com/TheAngelMicrobrewery).

Ian pointed out to me that in the past this venue was once a church and a whore house.

## Dinner

Once we'd sound checked and helped Sunscreem bring their equipment up Ian took me out for dinner, we were going to wait for Simon but his train was delayed.

We went round the corner to [Mesa](https://www.mesahockley.co.uk) between we had:

- Pickled plums with smoked vinaigrette
- Braised pork jowl with mojo rojo, almond and banana mole, pickles romaine
- ex-dairy cheeseburger with bone marrow sauce, dill pickles
- fries with miso and confit garlic dip & house chip spice

The food was amazing and you should try and go if you are in Nottingham, but book ahead we were lucky to get a table.

## Club Onion

### Sunscreem

It has been a very long time since I have seen [Sunscreem](https://sunscreem.com/) live, I'd say around 1993(ish). They were really good and it was delightful to see just how happy they were playing together. I'm really looking forward to see them again later in the year.

[Video of Sunscreem performing Perfect Motion](https://vimeo.com/1182281838?share=copy&fl=sv&fe=ci)

### Support

The support tonight was great and included:

- DJ Indigo Herder (whom I missed as I was eating)
- [Steve Hardaker](https://stevehardaker.bandcamp.com/)
- [Filter Sequence](https://filtersequence.bandcamp.com/) (featuring me on Guest Vocals for [More Acid](https://song.link/i/1874160407))
- [James Prail](https://jamesprail.bandcamp.com/)

What a great night, loads of people in the crowd came and told me they really enjoyed my performance/shouting, I had a great time time doing it.
