Last night I went to [The Moth Club](https://mothclub.co.uk/), with my friends Lisa & Steven, to see Harry Hill.

The Moth Club runs a comedy night called [Knock2Bag](https://www.knock2bag.com/) which are really affordable, attract huge stars, promote up and coming comedians and is a small venue.

## Harry Hill

As to be expected Harry Hill was superb, so much better than when you see him on the TV. He came out and immediately said:

> That JK Rowling, he's not very nice is he?

Superb!

## Other comedians

There were other comedians last night all very funny, my highlight was [Ele McKenzie](https://www.instagram.com/ele__mckenzie/), who I thought I recognised but couldn't place until I looked her up and she was a star in [Funboys](https://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/m0027p6q).

## Moth Club

The Moth Club, is a great community venue for Music, Comedy & club night and is currently under threat so if you could [sign the petition](https://www.change.org/p/moth-club-faces-second-building-planning-application) that would be awesome.
